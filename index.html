Casa Mercy is an ideal accommodation option in Varadero, situated within 30 meters of one of the world's most beautiful beaches and on the city's premier Avenue. We offer two independent rooms, each with a double and a single bed, accommodating a maximum of three persons per room.

Fotos

Our rooms are equipped with a range of amenities to ensure your comfort and convenience during your stay. Each room features a TV, split air conditioning and fan, refrigerator, closet, safe and a private bathroom with hot water. Stay connected with our 24-hour WiFi, and enjoy exceptional customer service throughhout your stay with us. We also offer daily room cleaning to ensure your stay is always fresh and comfortable.

Guests can enjoy a breathtaking view of the beach from our rooftop, which is shared by both rooms. We are also conveniently located near several points of interest, including ETECSA, where you can purchase nauta cards for WiFi, the photographic viewpoint of Varadero, and the house of Havano, where you can purchase souvenirs of your visit to this turistic area. Within a radius of less than 100 meters, you will find several restaurants and cafes that have earned their reputation for exceptional offers and customer service, including Don Alex, Salsa Suarez, and El Barco.
